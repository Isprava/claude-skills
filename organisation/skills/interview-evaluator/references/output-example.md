# Output Example Reference

This file shows the quality bar for the interview-evaluator skill output.
It documents the structure of a well-executed output for a real case:
Role = AI Execution PM Associate | Candidate = Keerti Sharma (B.Com, Power BI, SQL, Python, no AI exposure)

## What good prose context looks like (before scorecard)

"Her projects are all visualization and reporting — not execution. A Sales Dashboard in Power BI 
and a Netflix SQL query are consumption-of-data tasks, not build-and-ship tasks. That's not a 
disqualifier, but it means the Execution dimension is the one you genuinely don't know the answer 
to yet — the resume can't tell you.

The B.Com angle works for or against her depending on one thing: Commerce graduates who end up 
technical did so because they were curious and self-driven, not because the curriculum pushed them 
there. If she can tell you why she learned SQL and Python, that's a strong signal.

The AI gap is real but not fatal. The question isn't 'does she know AI?' — it's 'will she know 
enough AI in 90 days to be useful?' That
