# Lohono Stays — Brand Reference Guide

Quick reference for all Lohono Stays marketing skills. This is the authoritative brand context used across all six Lohono skills.

## Brand Overview

**Lohono Stays** is a premium luxury private villa rental brand offering handpicked villas across India and internationally.

**Brand promise:** "Handpicked homes, paired with unparalleled hospitality."

## Voice & Tone

### Tone pillars
1. **Aspirational** — Elevates the idea of what a stay can be. Not aspirational in a cold or status-obsessed way, but in a way that makes the reader feel the experience is genuinely worth wanting.
2. **Warm** — Feels like a recommendation from a friend who has been there. Not a hotel brochure. Conversational without being casual.
3. **Sensory** — Shows, doesn't tell. Uses specific details — a sound, a smell, a light quality — rather than adjectives.
4. **Experiential** — Focuses on what the guest does, feels, and takes home — not on features, square footage, or specifications.

### Banned words and phrases
Never use in any Lohono content:
- world-class
- luxury redefined
- unmatched (in any form)
- best-in-class
- nestled
- boasting
- seamless experience
- bespoke journey
- curated memories
- breathtaking
- stunning
- exquisite
- idyllic
- pristine
- don't miss out
- book now and save
- limited time offer (unless there is genuinely a timed offer)
- leveraged, synergies, holistic (corporate jargon)

### Banned patterns
- Exclamation marks for excitement (!)
- Opening with a question as a hook
- Vague descriptors in place of specific sensory detail
- Selling features when you could be painting moments
- Generic claims that could apply to any luxury brand

## Audience Profiles

### Primary domestic audience
- **Mumbai:** High-earning couples, founders, creatives, bankers. Travel-savvy. Weekend-driven. Short on time, long on discernment.
- **Delhi:** HNIs, business families, government and corporate leadership. Value status and discretion equally.
- **Bangalore:** Startup founders, senior tech leaders, international-minded professionals. Value experience and authenticity over optics.
- **Hyderabad:** New-money and old-money HNIs. Family travel dominant. Value privacy and quality of service.

### International audience (for Bali, Phuket, Italy)
- Primarily English-speaking couples and small groups
- Familiar with Airbnb Luxe, Booking.com Homes, and One&Only-level properties
- Selecting Lohono for the handpicked assurance + hospitality layer that self-service villa platforms don't offer

## Audience Segments by Occasion

| Segment | Core motivation | What they fear | What converts them |
|---------|----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Honeymooners** | Intimacy, a perfect beginning | "Will it be special enough?" | Seeing the specific detail — a bed set up on the terrace, a sunrise breakfast, a couple who went before them |
| **Anniversaries / milestones** | Recapture and celebrate | "Will it match the first time?" | Evidence of personal touches; the feeling that it was chosen for them, not just available |
| **Families** | Connection + children's joy + adult rest | "Will there be enough for everyone?" | Pool, space, a chef who does kids' food, something for the adults that isn't just watching the children |
| **Corporate retreats** | Focus, team cohesion, status justification | "Can I justify this to the CFO?" | ROI language + aspirational imagery; the idea of outcomes in the right setting |
| **Harmony Weddings** | Flawless execution of the most important day | "What if something goes wrong?" | Testimonials, a walk-through of how Lohono manages everything, the reassurance of a dedicated planner |
| **Lohono Infinity members** | Recognition, a step up on the last stay | "Will they remember me?" | Explicit acknowledgment of their loyalty; access to something not available to new guests |
